Policy

AI Policy Perspectives

With most technology, AI faces the debate between regulation for safety and freedom to conquer for rapid advancement. This page dives into the critical differences between the US and EU on AI and data regulation policy issues. Through side-by-side comparisons, you'll learn how each region tackles these challenges, and more importantly, what they prioritize and what it means for the future of AI.

1. Privacy and Data Protection

EU Stance

The GDPR views and treats privacy as a fundamental human right. Under it, companies need to get clear consent for using personal data, and enforces strict transparency, even for non-EU companies.1

EU Policy
US Stance

The US lacks a comprehensive federal data protection law like the GDPR, and instead relies on a patchwork of state laws and sector-specific regulations.2

US Policy

2. Algorithmic Transparency

EU Stance

GDPR requires companies to explain automated decisions and requires risk assessments for high-risk AI processes.1

EU Policy
US Stance

The Executive Order requires intensive testing for AI to identify risks like bias or misuse and requires tools like watermarking for AI-generated content.2

US Policy

3. Copyright and AI-Generated Content

EU Stance

The EU's AI Act and Digital Services Act propose strict regulations for AI-generated content, requiring clear labeling and transparency about AI involvement.1

EU Policy
US Stance

US copyright law is evolving to address AI-generated content. The Copyright Office has issued guidance stating that AI-generated works without human authorship cannot be copyrighted.2

US Policy

4. AI Safety and Regulation

EU Stance

The EU's AI Act introduces a risk-based framework, and categorizes AI systems based on their potential harm. High-risk AI systems face strict requirements for safety, transparency, and human oversight.1

EU Policy
US Stance

Proposals for AI regulation in the US are currently more fragmented, with ongoing debates about the balance between innovation and regulation.2

US Policy
