History

Explore the past of AI

Learn how it started and where it is now.

Origins

Artificial Intelligence is born. Foundational figures like Alan Turing and others begin experimentation and design key theoretical frameworks.

AI Winter

Unrealistic expectations and limited progress leads to dwindling interest. The lack of results creates growing skepticism about the future of AI.

Research and Growth

Promising results despite lack of funding reignites interest in AI. Ideas, old and new, are implemented and advance the field rapidly.

Industry Impact

AI systems moved from research labs to practical use, cementing its place in industry mostly away from the eyes of the general public.

Going Mainstream

The advent of groundbreaking AI tools leads to widespread adoption across various sectors. As awareness of this technology grows, discussions highlight both the hype and concerns surrounding AI and its future.
